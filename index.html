Bracknell Masjid

حَدَّثَنَا عَبْدُ اللَّهِ بْنُ يُوسُفَ، قَالَ أَخْبَرَنَا مَالِكٌ، عَنْ نَافِعٍ

عَنْ عَبْدِ اللَّهِ بْنِ عُمَرَ، أَنَّ رَسُولَ اللَّهِ صلى الله عليه وسلم قَالَ صَلاَةُ الْجَمَاعَةِ تَفْضُلُ صَلاَةَ الْفَذِّ بِسَبْعٍ وَعِشْرِينَ دَرَجَةً ‏

Ibn `Umar (May Allah be pleased with him) reported: The Messenger of Allah (sallallaahu ’alayhi wa sallam) said, “Salat in congregation is twenty-seven times more meritorious than a Salat performed individually.”

[Al-Bukhari and Muslim]

News & Events

Assalamualaykum wa rahmat Allah wa barakatu

Alhamdulillah we are happy to announce that we will be offering a weekly beginners’ tajweed class for sisters starting on Thursday February 7, 2015 at the Masjid. The class will run from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM in sha’ Allah.

While no previous knowledge of tajweed is required, a working knowledge of the Arabic language is necessary - you do not need to be fluent in the Arabic language.

We look forward to seeing you on Thursday in sha’ Allah. Jazakum Allah Khairn
